Registration

All talks are upon invitation only and in a single track (i.e., no parallel sessions). We aim for about 60 participants in total and will foresee plenty of time for discussions.

The registration fee for non-speakers is 150 EUR, to be paid via bank transfer. This fee includes the conference dinner on Thursday July 10th evening. Please use the following google form to register: Registration form.
