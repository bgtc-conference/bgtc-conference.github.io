Travel

By Plane

Take a flight to Brussels National Airport (Zaventem) or Charleroi Airport (Brussels South).

Brussels Airport (IATA code: BRU)

About 10km from Brussels. We recommend to choose this airport.

Charleroi Airport (IATA code: CRL)

About 60km from Brussels. There are buses making the connection between the airport and the Brussels railway station "Gare du midi" (South station). It takes around 2 hours.

By train

You can plan a national journey on http://www.belgianrail.be/en/.
You can plan an international journey on http://www.b-europe.com/Travel/
(From = your location, To = Gent-Sint-Pieters, set your date and time).

By car

The ULB parking on the campus is not accessible to visitors unfortunately. There is a street on the campus with paid parking which is 2' walk from the conference building: Avenue Roger Lallemand.

Accommodation

The conference is located on Campus Plaine of ULB. This is not to be confused with the main campus of ULB (Solbosch), which is 15' walk away. See the following Google Maps link for the precise location of the conference's building. Nearby, there is only one hotel, a recently built hotel next to Hospital Delta: Adagio Delta ( https://g.co/kgs/r7bkQTh ). It's 5' walk to the conference. Another good option is to book an airbnb nearby. Alternatively, we recommend staying around the Flagey neighborhood (35' walk / 25' by bus to the conference), which is a lively and trendy neighborhood offering many nice restaurants and bars. Yet another option is to stay in the city center and take the subway to the campus (Metro stop Delta, it takes about 45').
